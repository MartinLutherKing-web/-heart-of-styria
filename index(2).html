Our Story

Where Austria Meets the Red Sea

A Hidden Gem in Hurghada

Austria
×
Egypt

Heart of Styria — formerly known as Mr. Hans — is Hurghada's most beloved Austrian bakery and restaurant. We bring the soulful flavours of Styria, Austria's greenest heartland, to the sun-drenched shores of the Egyptian Red Sea.

For too long, Hurghada lacked the kind of place where you could sit down to a proper Wiener Frühstück, enjoy a hand-rolled croissant, or treat yourself to a warm Apfelstrudel with cream. We changed that — and we haven't looked back since.

Herzblut in jedem Detail
"Heart in every detail" — the philosophy behind everything we make. From the first loaf baked at dawn to the last coffee served at night.
Baked Fresh Daily
Every bread, every roll, every pastry — prepared in-house from scratch each morning. No shortcuts, ever.

Authentic Taste,
Honest Prices

In a town where European restaurant prices have skyrocketed, we made a different choice. Heart of Styria offers outstanding quality — authentic Austrian Mehlspeisen, hearty main dishes, and specialty baked goods — at prices that respect our guests.

Whether you're a tourist enjoying a well-deserved holiday or a Hurghada resident who simply wants a taste of home, this is your place.
