What we bring to the table

A slice of the Steiermark on the Red Sea

Three counters, one heart: a bakery for your daily bread, a kitchen for hearty Austrian comfort food, and a deli for breakfast on the go.

Bäckerei

Bauernbrot, Kornspitz, Laugenbrötchen and more — hand-shaped breads and rolls baked fresh every morning, plus pretzels, Stangen and party breads.

Hauptspeisen

Classic Austrian mains — Rindsschnitzel, Gulasch, Steirisches Cordon Bleu, Käsespätzle and a full vegetarian selection, served with care.

Frühstück & Theke

Start the day right with a Kleines or Großes Frühstück, fresh Apfelstrudel, toasts and a warm or cold deli counter for snacks on the move.
