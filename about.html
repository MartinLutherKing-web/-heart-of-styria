Our Story

Made with heart, baked with care

A new Austrian bakery and restaurant on the Red Sea, bringing the traditions of the Steiermark — the "Green Heart of Austria" — to Hurghada.

Where the name comes from

From Steiermark to the Red Sea

"Styria" is the English name for Steiermark, an Austrian region known as the Green Heart of Austria — famous for its forests, farms and a proud baking tradition passed down through generations. Heart of Styria brings that tradition to Hurghada: a working bakery counter alongside a full Austrian kitchen, all under one roof in the Touristic Villages area.

From the first Brötchen out of the oven in the morning to the last Schnitzel served at night, everything is prepared fresh, in-house, every single day.

A day at Heart of Styria

From morning bread to evening Schnitzel

Whatever time you visit, there's something fresh waiting for you — here's a glimpse of how a typical day unfolds at our counters.

Morning — Fresh from the oven

Bauernbrot, Kaisersemmel, Laugenbrötchen and sweet Zopf come out of the oven, ready for a Kleines or Großes Frühstück.

Midday — Soups, salads & deli

Hearty soups, fresh salads and the warm and cold deli counters keep things moving for a quick lunch or snack.

Evening — Hauptspeisen

Gulasch, Rindsschnitzel, Cordon Bleu and Käsespätzle take center stage, with Apfelstrudel or Kaiserschmarrn to finish.

Anytime — Kebab Station & Party Breads

For something different, our Kebab Station and pre-orderable Party-Brote are ready whenever the craving hits.
